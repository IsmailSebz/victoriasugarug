Sugar

Victoria Sugar produces premium quality refined and raw sugar from freshly harvested sugar cane, processed in our state-of-the-art mill in Luwero District, Uganda.

Our sugar is UNBS certified and ISO 9001:2015 compliant. We supply retail, food & beverage manufacturers, pharmaceutical companies, and export markets.
