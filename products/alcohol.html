Extra Natural Alcohol

Victoria Sugar's Extra Natural Alcohol (ENA) is a high-purity neutral spirit distilled from fermented sugar cane molasses. It is colourless, odourless, and meets international standards.

Our ENA is used by leading beverage manufacturers, cosmetics producers, and pharmaceutical companies across Uganda and the region.
