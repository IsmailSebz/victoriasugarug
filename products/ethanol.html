Overview

Ethanol

Victoria Sugar produces fuel-grade and industrial ethanol from fermented molasses. Our ethanol supports Uganda's biofuel blending mandate and provides a clean, renewable alternative to fossil fuels.

Available in hydrated and anhydrous grades, our ethanol meets international fuel standards and is supplied to blending plants, industrial users, and research institutions.
