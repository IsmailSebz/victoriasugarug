Electricity

Victoria Sugar operates a bagasse co-generation power plant that converts sugar cane waste (bagasse) into clean, renewable electricity, powering our factory and exporting surplus to Uganda's national grid.

Our co-generation plant makes our operations energy self-sufficient and contributes to Uganda's power supply — powering thousands of homes in surrounding communities.
