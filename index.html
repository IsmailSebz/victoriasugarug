Who We Are

Uganda's Premier Sugar & Agro-Industrial Company

Founded in 1999, Victoria Sugar Limited is one of Uganda's leading agro-industrial companies, headquartered in Luwero District. We grow, harvest, and process sugar cane into premium sugar, extra neutral alcohol, ethanol, and electricity — all from a single, integrated facility.

Our operations directly support over 5,000 smallholder farmers through our outgrower programme, contributing to food security and economic development across Uganda.

  • ISO 9001:2015 & FSSC 22000 certified food safety standards
  • Exporting to 10+ African & international markets
  • Fully integrated renewable energy generation
  • Committed to community development and outgrower support
