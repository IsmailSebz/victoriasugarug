Who We Are

Uganda's Premier Sugar & Agro-Industrial Company

Victoria Sugar Limited is one of Uganda's leading agro-industrial companies, headquartered in Luwero District. We grow, harvest, and process sugar cane into premium sugar, extra neutral alcohol, ethanol and electricity — all from a single, integrated facility.

As a consumer-driven business, VSL takes time to study the market and understand consumer needs and wants as well as how best to satisfy them. That is why we constantly invest in innovating and renovating our brands to stay at par with dynamic consumer trends. This goes hand in hand with our Vision to be the most celebrated business in Uganda.

Our Commitment

Sustainability & Responsibility

We grow not just sugar cane, but sustainable futures for Uganda's people and planet.

Sustainable Farming

We use water-efficient irrigation, crop rotation, and organic composting to maintain soil health and minimize environmental impact.

Community Development

From schools to health centres, we invest in the communities around our operations — building infrastructure that lasts beyond business.

Renewable Energy

Our bagasse co-generation plant produces clean electricity from sugar cane waste, supplying the national grid and surrounding villages.
