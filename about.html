Who We Are

Built on Sugar Cane, Rooted in Uganda

Victoria Sugar Limited was established with a vision to become one of Africa's leading agro-industrial enterprises. Located at Plot No. 7, Ndibulungi Village, Luwero District, our integrated facility produces sugar, extra neutral alcohol, fuel ethanol, and renewable electricity all from sugar cane.

Our market-specific strategies allow us the ability to identify and shape consumer trends, as well as respond to market dynamics to support growth. We are a proud Sugarcane to sugar business and remain steadfastly focused on.

Sourcing and producing locally where viable to support local communities. Playing a positive role in society and delivering value to our stakeholders including employees, customers and shareholders.

Our Journey

25+ Years of Growth

1998

Founded

Victoria Sugar Limited established in Luwero District, Uganda, with a vision to grow Uganda's sugar sector.

2004

First Harvest

First commercial sugar harvest processed, marking the start of our commercial production journey.

2010

Ethanol Plant

Commissioned our ethanol distillery, expanding product range to include fuel ethanol and extra neutral alcohol.

2015

ISO Certification

Achieved ISO 9001:2015 and FSSC 22000 certification — confirming our commitment to international quality standards.

2019

Co-generation Power

Launched our bagasse co-generation electricity plant, supplying clean power to the national grid.

2023

Export Expansion

Expanded exports to 10+ countries, solidifying our position as a major regional agro-industrial player.

2026

Presidential Visit

Officially oprnrd by the president of Uganda, President Yoweri Museveni.
