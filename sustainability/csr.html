Corporate Social Responsibility

At Victoria Sugar, CSR is embedded in how we operate. We believe that business success and community wellbeing are inseparable. Our CSR programmes touch education, health, environment, and economic empowerment.

Education Support

We fund scholarships, construct classrooms, and provide school materials to children in Luwero District and beyond.

Healthcare Access

Our medical outreach programmes bring free health services to remote communities around our operations.

Environmental Stewardship

Tree planting, water conservation, and soil health programmes to protect the land we depend on.

Economic Empowerment

Supporting women-led farmer groups, youth employment, and skills training for local communities.
