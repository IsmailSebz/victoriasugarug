Workplace Safety

Health & Safety

The safety of our 3,000+ employees is our highest operational priority. Our OHSAS 18001 certified Health & Safety Management System ensures that every worker goes home safely every day.

Safety Training

All employees receive regular safety inductions, refresher training, and emergency response drills.

PPE & Equipment

Full Personal Protective Equipment issued to all operational staff. Regular equipment inspection and maintenance.

Incident Management

Zero-tolerance for unsafe practices. All incidents are reported, investigated, and lessons shared company-wide.

Medical Support

On-site medical facility with trained nurses and doctors. Emergency evacuation protocols in place.
