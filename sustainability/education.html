Investing in Futures

Education Initiatives

Education is the most powerful tool for sustainable development. Victoria Sugar invests in the education of children and youth in the communities where we operate, building the foundation for Uganda's future.

Scholarship Programme

Annual scholarships for children of outgrower farmers, enabling bright students to access quality secondary and university education.

School Infrastructure

Construction and renovation of classrooms, libraries, and sanitation facilities in local schools.

Skills & Vocational Training

Partnerships with BTVET institutions to train young people in agri-business, mechanics, and food processing.
